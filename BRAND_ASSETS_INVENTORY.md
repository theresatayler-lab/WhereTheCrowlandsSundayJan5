## Asset Organization Tips

### Opacity Recommendations:
- **Hero backgrounds**: 10-15%
- **Section backgrounds**: 3-5%
- **Decorative dividers**: 50-60%
- **Card overlays**: 5-8%

### Sizing Guidelines:
- **Divider engravings**: h-16 to h-24 (64px-96px)
- **Hero backgrounds**: Full cover
- **Texture overlays**: bg-cover or bg-repeat
- **Icons**: h-6 to h-12 (24px-48px)

### Blend Modes:
- **multiply**: For dark engravings on light backgrounds (current)
- **overlay**: For subtle texture integration
- **darken**: For maintaining engraving details
