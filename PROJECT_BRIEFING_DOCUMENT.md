## PROJECT OVERVIEW

### What Is It?

**Where The Crowlands** is a practical toolkit that empowers people to build their own rituals and spells using tested historical formulas.

**Core Value Proposition:**
> "You don't need an Etsy witch or tarot reader to access your power. Build your own practice with formulas, sacred geometry, and intentional effort. Magic is scienceâ€”patterns and shapes we put into practice."

### Target Audience

**Primary Users:**
- DIY practitioners who reject gatekeeping
- People interested in ritual as psychological tool
- Those seeking practical frameworks (not mysticism)
- Individuals who want to build their own practices
- Skeptics open to experimentation

**NOT For:**
- People seeking religious guidance
- Those wanting mystical experiences
- Users looking for fortune-telling services
- People who want others to do magic "for them"

### Positioning

**We Are:** A practical toolkit (like a cookbook for rituals)  
**We Are Not:** A religious site, fortune-telling service, or mystical guru

**Key Differentiators:**
1. Anti-gatekeeping philosophy
2. Magic as science/patterns approach
3. Historical rigor (1910-1945 sources)
4. DIY empowerment focus
5. No belief required

## KEY LEARNINGS & INSIGHTS

### What We Discovered During Development

1. **Dark backgrounds don't work** with black engravings
   - Needed to pivot to light parchment
   - This actually improved authenticity

2. **Historical accuracy is valued**
   - Users want real sources
   - Honesty about disputes builds trust
   - Citing references is important

3. **Anti-gatekeeping resonates**
   - Strong response to empowerment messaging
   - "You don't need permission" is powerful
   - Positioning as science vs. mysticism works

4. **Visual storytelling matters**
   - Engravings communicate more than text
   - Image-driven design creates immersion
   - Authentic assets beat stock photos

5. **Simplicity is hard**
   - Started with 8+ nav items (overwhelming)
   - Need to ruthlessly prioritize
   - Less is more for new users

### Design Principles Established

1. **Authenticity Over Decoration**
   - Use real historical imagery
   - Cite actual sources
   - Be honest about what's known vs. disputed

2. **Empowerment Over Gatekeeping**
   - Give tools, don't hoard knowledge
   - Encourage adaptation, not rigid following
   - Free access, no paywalls for core features

3. **Science Over Mysticism**
   - Frame as patterns and formulas
   - Explain mechanisms
   - No "woo-woo" language

4. **Images Tell Stories**
   - Every engraving has purpose
   - Visual hierarchy through imagery
   - Let art communicate tone

5. **Function Over Fashion**
   - Beautiful, but usable
   - Clear CTAs
   - Readable text
   - Fast performance
