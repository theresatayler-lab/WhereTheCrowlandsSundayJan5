## Design Direction Based on Your Images

### Color Scheme Adjustment Needed:
Your images are **black and white woodcuts** - they need a lighter background!

**Recommended Background Colors:**
```css
Option 1: Weathered Parchment
background: #e8dcc8 (cream/parchment)
text: #1a1410 (dark brown)
accents: #750609 (blood red), #06133c (midnight blue)

Option 2: Aged Paper
background: #d8cbb8 (weathered beige - your brand color!)
text: #2a2520 (warm dark)
accents: #750609, #06133c

Option 3: Light Warm Grey
background: #e0d5c7
text: #1C1C1C (raven black)
accents: #750609, #06133c
```

**Why lighter background?**
- Your engravings are BLACK on WHITE originally
- They'll pop beautifully on cream/parchment
- Creates "ancient grimoire" aesthetic
- More authentic to historical occult books
